Marketing/Technical Content Writer

  • Position: Marketing/Technical Content Writer
  • Type: Full-time
  • Location: Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia

About us

erxes is a free and open fair-code licensed all-in-one growth marketing & management software. We offer a single solution for sales, marketing, and customer service teams, focusing on the entire customer experience.

About this role

This role is all about powering our growth funnel with persuasive copywriting. You will be working directly with the product team to support inbound lead generation, outbound campaigns, product launches, and other high-growth initiatives by creating engaging content that attracts, converts, engages, and retains users.

Responsibilities

  • Coordinate between cross-functional teams to write copy for various marketing asset types, including help guides, executive briefs, web copy, and blogs.
  • Conduct thorough research on our industry-related topics, generating ideas for new content types and proofreading articles before publication.
  • Write clear marketing copy to promote our products/services.
  • Use project management and content management software platforms as needed.
  • Coordinate with marketing and design teams to illustrate articles.
  • Using identified keyword list, SEO optimizes all written copy.
  • Identify target audience needs and gaps in our content and recommend new topics.
  • Ensure all-around consistency (style, fonts, images, and tone).
  • Update website content as needed.

What we’re looking for

  • Demonstrated success working as a Content Writer, Copywriter, or similar role – with a portfolio link or writing samples provided with the application.
  • Fluency in English is a must.
  • A self-directed, quick learner with the ability to work collaboratively and cross-functionally. 
  • Strong ethical compass and integrity; act with the utmost discretion at all times. No shady marketing gimmicks.
  • Extremely organized with attention to detail.

What we offer

  • Compensation of 1,600,000 – 3,200,000 MNT/month depending on skills and experience
  • Work from home: Decide on your hours and work from home or remotely whenever you like.
